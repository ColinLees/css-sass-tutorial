Report a change of address

You can use this form if you're:

  • moving into the Stockport area
  • moving from one address to another in the Stockport area
  • moving out of the Stockport area
  • a Stockport resident, currently not liable for Council Tax, for example: if you are living with your parents and moving into your own property
  • a landlord or letting agent advising us of a tenant(s) moving in or out
  • selling your property and you'll have the 'bill of sale' in the next 2 weeks

Before you start

You'll need to know:

  • your Council Tax account number if you are moving around or out of Stockport
  • details of the property you're leaving
  • details of the property you're moving to
  • dates of your move
  • the full name and the date of birth of all residents aged 18 or over at the property you're moving to

How we use your data

You're telling us about a change of address and we're legally required to share this information with our Elections team to keep our voting register up to date. The information we share with them is the:

  • main applicant’s name and date of birth
  • name of any other residents aged over 18 year within your home
  • address of the old property
  • address of the new property
  • main applicant’s email address if you give consent for us to share this on the form

We primarily use your personal data for the purpose of changing your address. We may also share your personal data with other local authorities and/or government departments in accordance with our organisational privacy notice. We will never sell your data to third parties, however, we may use your personal data to contact you about council services that we think may be of interest to you.
